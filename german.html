Start Learning German Today!

German is a widely spoken language with over 100 million people in countries such as Austria, and Switzerland. German is also widely spoken in other Eureopean countries and one of the ten most spoken languages in the world.

German is not as difficult for English speakers to pick up on since English is also a Germanic language. Also, many words in English are similar to German, so as an English speaker it will be easier to learn than other languages. Unlike Chinese, Russian or Korean, the German's alphabet will look similar to English.

German is spoken in the business world since many companies have international locations with lots of international business occurring in Europe. You may have more opportunity by knowing German.

Germany has a high standing in the science community, so if you know German you may be able to study abroad at a German university. Engineering, medicine and philosophy are popular topics to study in Germany and a degree from a German university could be valued by employers worldwide.
