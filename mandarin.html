Why Learn Mandarin Chinese?

China is the world’s third-largest country with over 1 billion people. Mandarin Chinese is currently spoken by nearly one-fifth of the world’s population. Mandarin speakers can be found in Mainland China, Taiwan, and other Chinese communities throughout Southeast Asia, North and South America, and Europe.

The Chinese culture dates back over 5,000 years of history and by learning the language, you are able to appreciate and understand China’s rich culture.

The Chinese language is in great demand due to the growth in business overseas. There are many professional opportunities for those interested in working in China and having proficiency in the language. International businesses prefer to hire people who are able to speak more than one language and you can bridge the gap by knowing Mandarin Chinese.

You can make new friends by speaking Mandarin Chinese with the ability to communicate with people all around the world!
